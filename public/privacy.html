Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information Alwan collects, how we use it, and how we protect it. Because Alwan is a mental wellness app, we take the handling of your personal data especially seriously.

Your journal entries are private by default. Organization admins do not have access to your personal diary content.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Alwan, we may collect the following:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and profile photo from Google or Facebook authentication
  • Username — the display name you choose within your team workspace
  • Mood data — mood check-ins and emotional logs you submit
  • Journal entries — personal diary entries you write within the app
  • Self-care activity data — activities you track and complete
  • Saved quotes — motivational quotes you archive
  • Organization data — the team workspace you belong to and your role
  • Device and usage data — browser type, device info, and app interaction data for performance and debugging
  • Push notification tokens — if you enable notifications, we store a device token to deliver reminders

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide and personalize your Alwan experience
  • Display your mood history, journal entries, and self-care progress
  • Send wellness reminders and push notifications (only if enabled)
  • Enable team features within your organization workspace
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Maintain the security and integrity of the platform

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Alwan stores data using:

  • Firebase (Google) — for authentication and real-time features
  • MongoDB — for storing mood logs, journals, self-care progress, and organization data
  • Local storage — for offline caching and draft journal entries on your device

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties except:

  • Service providers necessary to operate the app (Firebase, MongoDB hosting)
  • When required by law or to protect the safety of users

Within your organization, admins may see aggregated or anonymized team wellness trends, but never your private journal content.

5. Push Notifications

If you grant notification permissions, Alwan may send you wellness reminders and self-care nudges. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings or within the app.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you leave an organization or delete your account, your personal data will be removed from our systems within a reasonable timeframe. Some anonymized data may be retained for analytics purposes.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of push notifications at any time

To exercise these rights, contact your organization admin or reach out to us through the app.

8. Children's Privacy

Alwan is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, we will delete it promptly.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS), Firebase Authentication, and access controls. However, no system is 100% secure, and we encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your linked Google or Facebook account.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. Continued use of Alwan after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about your privacy, please contact us through the app or speak with your organization admin.
